Prismatic

Prismatic Battery

A Prismatic Battery is a type of battery with a rectangular or prism-like shape, most commonly used in hybrid and electric vehicles. It is designed to optimize energy density and space by offering a larger surface area than cylindrical batteries. Prismatic batteries are well-suited for applications requiring compact and lightweight energy sources.

Lithium Phosphate

Lithium-ion batteries have certain drawbacks that make lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) a better alternative. Even though LiFePO4 batteries are also lithium-ion batteries there are many diverse variations in the chemistry of this lithium battery

Lithium-ion

A lithium-ion or Li-ion batteries are a type of rechargeable battery. They are popular for their higher energy density. They weight less than 30 gms, higher specific energy, excellent energy efficiency, and durability with a longer cycle life.
